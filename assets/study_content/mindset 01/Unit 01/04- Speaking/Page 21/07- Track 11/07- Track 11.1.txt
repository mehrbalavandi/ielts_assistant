Hoi Chin: My family? Well, my family isn't a large family. It's quite a small family in fact - and quite a typical family for my country. Just my parents, my older brother and me. So, I'm the baby of the family! I think we're a close, happy family. We do a lot of things together, particularly preparing food - and eating it of course! Yes, we spend a lot of time in the kitchen. My father's a very good cook - he's much better than my mother.
My brother's getting married next year, so I'm really looking forward to having a sister-in-law! And who knows? Perhaps our family will get bigger in the future! I'd love to have a niece or a little nephew to take to the park one day. Yes, I really want to be an aunt!
