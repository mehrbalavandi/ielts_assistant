Complete the sentences about tea. Use the verbs in brackets in the present simple passive.

1 Tea leaves [~are picked~] (pick) by hand.
2 Only the best leaves [~are used~] (use).
3 A lot of water [~is needed ~] (need) to grow tea plants successfully. 
4 A lot of tea [~is grown ~] (grow) in China and India.
