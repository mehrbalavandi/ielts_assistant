⚫The modals con and could are used to talk about ability in the present and past.
Dolphins can tell when a woman is pregnant.
Yang Yun found she couldn't move her legs due to the freezing temperatures.

⚫Modal verbs may, might and could are used when something is possible but not certain:
Experts think dolphins may understand that humans are similar to them
