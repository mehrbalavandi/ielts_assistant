GRAMMAR FOCUS: FIRST CONDITIONAL
Look at this sentence from the passage 'Become rich and famous through the internet'.
If you want to become known on Twitter, you will need to post lots of entertaining content. This is called a first conditional. It has two clauses, an 'If' clause and a result clause. The 'If clause is in the present tense. The result clause has 'will / won't + infinitive'.
