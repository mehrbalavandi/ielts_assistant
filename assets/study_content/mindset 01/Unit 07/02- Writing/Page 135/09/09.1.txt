Read the essay title. Which of the statements a-d below best matches your opinion on the essay topic?
There should be strict laws banning people from taking photographs of people under 18 in order to protect young celebrities and the children of celebrities. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
a I strongly agree. Laws must be very strict to protect children,
b I tend to agree, It is probably not a good idea to allow photos of children.
c I tend to disagree. I think people are interested in seeing photos of famous children.
d I strongly disagree. People have the right to take pictures of whoever they want.

TIP 09
"To what extent do you agree or disagree?" means "How strongly do you agree or disagree? Use phrases like Istrongly agree, or I tend to disagree.
