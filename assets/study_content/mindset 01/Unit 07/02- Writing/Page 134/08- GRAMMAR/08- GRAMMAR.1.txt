GRAMMAR FOCUS: PRONOUNS

⚫Using pronouns is a way of avoiding repetition.
Examples
Lionel Messi is a famous footballer. Lionel Messi / He plays for Barcelona and Argentina. I can think of many drawbacks. The first drawback/one is the cost.
