Getting the best from your camcorder

Most camcorders can perform all necessary tasks using automatic functions.
But for a more professional result, you must also learn how to use the 1[~manual~] controls.
↓
The Focus function on the camera is very 2[~useful~]. You need to turn the focus ring one way or the other, depending on whether the object you want to film is 3[~closer~] or further away.
↓
The Iris is an adjustable opening (aperture) which controls the amount of 4[~light~] coming through the lens (the exposure). As you open the ins, more light comes in and the picture appears 5[~brighter~]
↓
Finally, the Zoom is a very 6[~popular~] manual function and therefore often over used. This feature moves your shot closer or further away from the subject. For 7[~long~] zooms a tripod is recommended.
